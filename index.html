About Us

SQR Meals initially started as a sustainable solution to the widespread unemployment around the world. We as a group of 4 socially inclined undergraduate students at IIT Kharagpur, realised that the scenario of youth unemployment in India is pretty grave and we need better approaches to fight poverty, unemployment and hunger, more than ever before.

Our Vision

Our Mission

We are committed to building a society where the destitute are not deprived of opportunities. A society where every labourer has access to a square meal and different communities are interlinked to lead sustainable lives.
We provide meaningful employment to orphaned youths and means of a healthy lifestyle to construction workers. This is to achieve our larger goal of supporting the socioeconomic integration of orphans as well as improving the productivity of daily labourers.

How Does It Work

We provide meaningful employment to orphaned youths through training in culinary, administration and maintenance. This training will enable them to run a business where we will provide catering services as a means of a healthy lifestyle to construction workers. This is to achieve our larger goal of supporting the socio-economic integration of orphans as well as improving the productivity of daily labourers and to interlink neglected communities : empowering them to live sustainable lives.

Projects

Our first milestone was the small pilot that we conducted on a construction site at IIT Kharagpur campus in March 2019. The “Square Meal” that we served cost just above INR 35. We catered to the dietary needs of around 20 construction workers providing them with a lunch meal that consisted of all the basic requirements like rice, a pulses curry, a vegetable curry, salad and chapatis. The impact of our venture was clearly visible from the smiles and satisfaction on their faces. We wish to expand reach beyond 20 people. The catering service will be provided by young orphans who are in search of honorable employment. Our aim is to integrate these two deprived communities for their mutual upliftment using affordable and sustainable methods.
